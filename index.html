Let's play a fun game of Four-in-a-Row!!

Four-in-a-Row is a classic strategy game that challenges players to connect a sequence of four of their colored discs in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally, within a grid. The game unfolds on a vertical standing board where participants take turns dropping one of their discs into any of the seven columns. The objective is to outsmart the opponent by strategically placing discs and blocking their attempts to create a winning alignment. With its simple yet engaging gameplay, Four-in-a-Row offers a delightful balance of skill and intuition, making it a timeless and enjoyable two-player experience. Rules of Four-in-a-Row:

  1. Two players take turns placing their colored discs on the vertical standing board.
  2. The board consists of seven columns and six rows.
  3. Players drop one disc at a time into any of the seven columns.
  4. The objective is to connect four of your colored discs in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
  5. A player wins the game as soon as they achieve a four-in-a-row formation.
  6. If the board is filled without a winner, the game ends in a draw.
  7. Players need to strategize to create winning alignments while blocking their opponent's moves.
  8. Turns alternate until a player wins, or the game reaches a draw.
